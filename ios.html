Steps to install Wash.io on your iPhone
(Please read these steps carefully before starting the installation.)


Step 1: Visit this page.

Step 2: Once the page has loaded, click the share icon in Safari.

Step 3: Scroll to the bottom of the share menu and click on 'Add to Home Screen'.

Step 4: In the confirmation popup, click on 'Add'.

You're all set to go! Use Wash.io on your iPhone just like any other app. No strings attached.
